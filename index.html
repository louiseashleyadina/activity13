Bachelor In Industrial Technology

A Bachelor of Science in Industrial Technology is an undergraduate degree awarded to students who have completed coursework in industrial technology. This degree prepares students for careers as industrial technologists: professionals who strive to improve productivity, profitability, and safety.

Program Object

The program is intended to prepare students for a professional Industrial Technologist career including a leading role in the production, manufacturing and enhancement of technology management.

Specifict Objectives

Industrial technology is the use of engineering and manufacturing technology to make production faster, simpler, and more efficient. The industrial technology field employs creative and technically proficient individuals who can help a company achieve efficient and profitable productivity.

Job Opportunities

  • Automation Engineer
  • Manufacturing Technician
  • Quality Control Inspector
  • Logistics and Supply Chain Manager
  • Industrial Designer
  • Environmental Health and Safety (EHS) Specialist
  • Project Manager
  • Operations Manager
